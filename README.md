## What Is GrowthDay, Really?

At its core, GrowthDay is three things stitched together: a daily habit system, a live coaching platform, and an AI life coach. The goal isn't to give you more content to consume — it's to get you to actually *do the thing* consistently.

Every day, you log into the app (mobile or browser, your choice) and work through the "5 to Thrive" dashboard:

- Listen to Brendon's **Daily Fire** — a fresh, never-repeating audio strategy that expires at midnight
- Write in your **mindset journal** with daily prompts
- Run through your **checklist** for the day
- Score your **daily habits** across six areas: Clarity, Energy, Necessity, Productivity, Influence, and Courage
- Track your **growth streak** — and yes, there's a leaderboard and monthly prizes

If you're someone who responds well to streaks (like the Duolingo crowd, but for your actual life), this format clicks.
